Check out our new Web-App for our new Campus!

Do you need to see the schedule for a specific room? check it out on our Web-App!
When you are in front of a classroom use the AR component of our web-app to see the schedule of the room via augmented reality.

In our web-app you will also find a virtual reality model over the new campus, giving you a realistic experience of the new building.

So all that is left to say is welcome to our new Campus!

The Web-App

Our Web-App is split in two parts. The Vr, & the AR. The VR component revolves around a virtual space where you can walk around the new campus, and get a good look of the building. You will also find some informative signs where you can read a bit about the new campus. The AR component is where we embed AR directly into the building, by having the room number designed as markers for the AR, when viewing the marker through the Web-App you will then see a Schedule for the room number you are checking out.

Virtual Reality

Virtual reality (VR) is a computer-generated scenario that simulates a realistic experience. The immersive environment can be similar to the real world in order to create a lifelike experience grounded in reality or sci-fi.

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality (AR) is a direct or indirect live view of a physical, real-world environment whose elements are "augmented" by computer-generated perceptual information, ideally across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory, and olfactory.
